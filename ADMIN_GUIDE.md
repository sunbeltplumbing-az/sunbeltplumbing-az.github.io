# Sunbelt Plumbing — Admin Guide

Hi Princess! 👋 This guide shows you how to update your website on your own. You don't need any technical skills — if you can use Facebook, you can use this.

---

## How to log in

1. Go to **https://sunbeltplumbing-az.github.io/admin/** (bookmark this!)
2. Type your business email and click **Send login link**
3. Open your email — there will be a message from Supabase
4. Click the big link in the email
5. You're in! ✨

You'll stay logged in on your computer/phone, so you usually won't need to do this more than once in a while.

---

## The Dashboard

The first page you see after logging in. It has three tiles:

- **Gallery** — photos of completed jobs
- **Testimonials** — customer reviews
- **Business Info** — phone, email, hours, etc.

Click any tile to manage that section.

---

## Adding photos to your gallery

This is probably what you'll do most often. Every time Shan finishes a nice job, snap a photo and add it here.

1. Click **Gallery** in the left menu
2. Drag a photo into the box that says "Drop an image here, or click to choose"
3. Add a short caption (optional, but helpful — e.g., *"New water heater install — Casa Grande"*)
4. Pick a category (Repair, Install, Emergency, Renovation, or Other)
5. Click **Save to gallery**
6. Done! The photo is now live on your website ✅

### Important photo tips

- **Use your phone's camera, not screenshots** — the quality is way better
- **Good lighting** makes photos look professional. Outdoor daylight is best.
- **Show the finished result clearly** — not mid-work clutter
- **Max size: 5 MB** — most phone photos are fine
- **iPhone users: ⚠️ IMPORTANT** — if uploads fail, change this setting:
  - Go to **Settings → Camera → Formats**
  - Choose **"Most Compatible"** (not "High Efficiency")
  - This makes your iPhone save photos as JPG instead of HEIC
- **Not allowed:** HEIC, BMP, GIF files. Only JPG, PNG, and WebP work.

### Rearranging photos

Each photo has **↑** and **↓** buttons. Click them to move a photo up or down in the gallery. The order on your admin page matches the order visitors see on the website.

### Editing a caption

Click **Edit** under any photo. Change the caption and save.

### Removing a photo

Click the red **×** button. You'll be asked to confirm. After confirming, the photo disappears from the website right away. (Don't worry — it's not permanently deleted from your storage, just hidden. If you ever need to recover something, your developer can help.)

## Adding testimonials (customer reviews)

Real customer reviews are gold for your website. Every time someone leaves a nice Google, Facebook, or text-message review, add it here.

1. Click **Testimonials** in the left menu
2. Click the blue **+ Add new review** button in the top right
3. Fill in:
   - **Customer name** — "Maria L." (just first name + last initial is fine)
   - **Location** — e.g., "Casa Grande" (optional)
   - **Service type** — e.g., "Water heater repair" (optional)
   - **Rating** — how many stars (almost always 5!)
   - **The review** — what the customer said (keep it under 500 characters)
4. Click **Save review**

### Editing or deleting a review

In the table, click **Edit** to change it, or **Delete** to remove it.

### Tip: getting more reviews

After every job, send a quick Messenger message like:
> *"Thanks so much for choosing Sunbelt! If you have a second, a Google review would mean the world to us: [link]"*

## Updating Business Info

Your phone, email, address, hours, etc. all live in one place.

1. Click **Business Info** in the left menu
2. Change any field you want
3. Click **Save changes**

Changes appear on the live site within a few seconds.

### What each field controls

- **Business phone** — shown in the header, footer, and "Call Now" buttons
- **Business email** — shown in the footer and contact page
- **Facebook page URL** — the Messenger icon and footer link
- **Operating hours** — shown on the contact page (e.g., "24-hour service")
- **Service area description** — short text on every page ("Arizona City & Casa Grande...")
- **Business address** — used for the Google Maps embed (leave blank to show a general area map)
- **Homepage tagline** — the big "We handle the pressure..." line
- **ROC license number** — shown as "ROC #344103" on every page

## Signing out

Click **Sign out** at the bottom of the left sidebar. You'll be taken back to the login page. Next time you come back, you'll need to request a login link again (same email process).

## Troubleshooting

### "I didn't get the login email"
- Check your **spam folder** — Supabase emails sometimes land there
- Wait 2 minutes and try again
- Make sure you typed the right email address

### "My photo won't upload"
- Check the file type — only **JPG, PNG, WebP** work (no HEIC from iPhone)
- Check the size — must be **under 5 MB**
- Try a different photo first to see if it's specific to that one

### "The website doesn't show my change"
- Wait about 30 seconds
- Hard-refresh: press **Ctrl + Shift + R** (or **Cmd + Shift + R** on Mac)
- If still not showing, message your developer

### "I accidentally deleted something"
- Don't panic! Deletes are actually "soft deletes" — the data is still there, just hidden
- Message your developer with what you deleted, and they can restore it

### "I forgot what email I used to log in"
- You used the business Gmail you created for Sunbelt
- If you truly can't remember, your developer can help you reset

## A few things NOT to do

- ❌ **Don't share your login email** with anyone who shouldn't have admin access
- ❌ **Don't click "Sign out" on a computer that isn't yours** and leave the tab open afterward
- ❌ **Don't upload photos of customers' personal belongings** (ID cards, mail, license plates) without permission
- ❌ **Don't paste links or code** from sources you don't recognize into any form
